# Skill: competitor_watch

**Agent**: Research (Nina)
**Priority**: P0

## Purpose

Monitor competitors of a SaaS product and provide actionable competitive intelligence.

## What to track per competitor

1. **Pricing changes** — any tier added, removed, or price changed
2. **Product updates** — new features announced (blog, changelog, release notes)
3. **Marketing moves** — new landing copy, positioning changes, new channels
4. **Funding rounds** — signals market validation and future capabilities
5. **Job postings** — indicates where they're investing (engineering, sales, support)
6. **Reviews** — G2/Capterra/Trustpilot: new complaints = your opportunity
7. **Social signals** — LinkedIn, Twitter/X engagement, community growth

## Monitoring sources

- Competitor blog/changelog RSS
- G2 / Capterra reviews (weekly)
- LinkedIn company page
- Twitter/X mentions
- Product Hunt for new launches in category
- Crunchbase funding alerts

## Output format (weekly report)

```
🔍 COMPETITOR WATCH — Week of [date]

[Competitor 1]
  → [change/signal + source + strategic implication]

[Competitor 2]
  → [change/signal + source + strategic implication]

KEY OPPORTUNITY: [one actionable insight from this week's intel]
THREAT: [one risk to flag to Sophie/Elena]
```

## When to escalate

- Competitor raises funding: immediate alert to Alex
- Competitor launches feature in your roadmap: urgent Sophie briefing
- Competitor cuts price significantly: immediate Elena briefing
