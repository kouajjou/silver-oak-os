# Skill: cold_outreach

**Agent**: Elena (Sales & Growth)
**Priority**: P0

## Purpose

Generate personalized cold email and LinkedIn outreach sequences for SaaS sales.

## Framework

### The 3-line cold email formula
1. **Trigger line** — specific observation about THEM (not your product)
2. **Value prop** — one sentence connecting their situation to your solution
3. **CTA** — one specific, low-commitment ask (15-min call? Yes/No?)

### Sequence structure (5 touches, 10 days)
- Day 1: Email 1 (trigger + value + CTA)
- Day 3: LinkedIn connection + note (reference email 1)
- Day 5: Email 2 (different angle, add social proof)
- Day 8: Email 3 (case study or result)
- Day 10: Break-up email ("Should I close your file?")

## Personalization research
For each prospect, find:
- Recent company news (funding, hire, product launch)
- Their LinkedIn activity (posts, comments they've made)
- Tech stack (use BuiltWith / Wappalyzer)
- Mutual connections or shared background

## Output format

Full 5-email sequence ready to copy-paste, with personalization placeholders marked as [PROSPECT_NAME], [COMPANY], [TRIGGER_OBSERVATION], etc.

## Guard-rails

- Subject line: max 6 words, no spam triggers (FREE, URGENT, !!!)
- Always include unsubscribe link (RGPD)
- Never pitch the product in email 1 — pitch the conversation
- Max 3 sentences per email
