# Skill: ph_launch

**Agent**: Elena (Sales & Growth)
**Priority**: P1

## Purpose

Prepare a complete Product Hunt launch package for a SaaS product.

## Deliverables

1. **Tagline** — 60 chars max, benefit-focused, not feature-focused
2. **Description** — 260 chars (PH limit), answers: what is it, who is it for, what pain does it solve
3. **First comment** — 800-1200 words: founder story, why built it, what makes it different, ask for feedback
4. **Gallery copy** — 5 screenshot captions (what each screen shows + the benefit)
5. **Hunter selection** — criteria for picking a hunter (10K+ followers, relevant audience)
6. **Launch day playbook** — hour-by-hour actions on launch day (6am-11pm PST)
7. **Upvote pre-commitment** — template DM to send to 50+ people before launch day

## Guard-rails

- Never launch Tuesday/Wednesday — Thursday or Friday for B2B
- Never launch without 30+ upvote commitments pre-secured
- Tagline must NOT contain "AI-powered", "revolutionary", or any buzzword
- First comment must include a genuine founder story (not marketing copy)
- Always schedule launch for 12:01am PST (midnight EST) to maximize voting window
