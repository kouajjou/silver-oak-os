# Skill: email_sequence

**Agent**: Elena (Sales & Growth)
**Priority**: P0

## Purpose

Create pre-launch waitlist and post-signup onboarding email sequences for a SaaS.

## Two sequences

### Pre-launch waitlist sequence (5 emails over 3 weeks)
1. **Welcome to the waitlist** (immediate) — confirm signup, set expectations, ask 1 question
2. **Behind the scenes** (day 3) — what we're building and why
3. **Early access sneak peek** (day 7) — screenshot or demo GIF
4. **Social proof** (day 14) — early user feedback / beta results
5. **Launch announcement** (day 21) — you're in! Here's your link.

### Post-signup onboarding sequence (7 emails over 14 days, J0-J14)
- **J0**: Welcome + get started (one action)
- **J1**: Did you [key action]? + help if not
- **J3**: Feature spotlight (the one that drives aha moment)
- **J7**: Aha moment check-in + social proof
- **J10**: Advanced feature or use case
- **J14**: Check-in + upgrade prompt (if freemium) or retention question

## Guard-rails

- Every email must have ONE call to action (not multiple)
- J7 email is critical — this is where most churn happens
- Always segment: activated (did key action) vs. not activated (didn't)
- Subject lines A/B tested: curiosity vs. direct benefit
