# Skill: email_seq

**Agent**: Content (Léo)
**Priority**: P0

## Purpose

Write the full lifecycle email sequence for a SaaS — from waitlist to long-term customer.

## Sequences

### 1. Welcome / onboarding (J0-J30)
See `email_sequence` skill under Elena for the detailed structure.
This version focuses on content quality and copywriting.

### 2. Feature announcement emails
- Subject: "New in [Product]: [Feature name]" (not "Product Update")
- Body: problem it solves → how it works (GIF/screenshot) → one-click CTA
- Max 150 words. Screenshots > words.

### 3. Monthly digest (power users)
- "Here's what you accomplished with [Product] in [Month]"
- Personalized stats: tasks done, time saved, money generated
- Makes users feel progress even if they've plateau'd

### 4. Win-back sequence (3 emails over 14 days)
- J0: "We noticed you haven't logged in — is everything ok?" (personal, not pushy)
- J7: "Here's what changed since you left" (feature update angle)
- J14: "Last chance — your data will be archived in 7 days" (urgency + save their data)

### 5. Upgrade nudge sequence
- When user hits usage limit: instant in-app + email
- After 2 consecutive months at 90% of limit
- Annual plan push in month 3 (when payback is proven)

## Copywriting rules

- Subject line: < 40 chars, no emojis in cold emails, one emoji max in warm
- Preheader: complements subject, don't repeat it
- Body: 3 paragraphs max, plain text > HTML for personal feel
- CTA: one per email, above the fold if long
- Signature: Karim (first name only), no title
