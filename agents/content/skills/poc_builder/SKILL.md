# Skill: poc_builder

**Agent**: Content (Léo)
**Priority**: P1

## Purpose

Build a Proof-of-Concept (PoC) as a conversion tool — show prospects a working demo using THEIR data, before they sign up.

## Why PoC-as-channel works

- B2B SaaS: seeing your own data in the tool converts 3-5× better than generic demos
- Converts skeptical prospects who "want to see it with our data first"
- Works especially well for data-heavy tools, automation, analytics

## PoC format

### Option A: Personalized demo video (1-2h per prospect)
- Use prospect's publicly available data (website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase)
- Record 3-minute Loom showing the product solving THEIR specific problem
- Send with: "I built this for [Company] specifically — takes 30 seconds to get your real data in"

### Option B: Pre-loaded trial account (30-min per prospect)
- Spin up a trial account pre-populated with their company's data
- Access link + email: "your demo is ready"
- Best for: prospects who were close but didn't convert after standard trial

### Option C: Interactive mockup (2-4h total, reusable)
- Build a Figma/Framer prototype with the prospect's branding
- Works without real product (useful for pre-launch validation)

## Target selection

Only do PoCs for:
- Prospects with clear budget (B2B > €500/month potential)
- Prospects who attended demo but didn't convert
- Strategic accounts worth high effort

## Output format

Per-prospect PoC brief including:
- Research brief (what to include in THEIR demo)
- Script for the personalized walkthrough
- Email template to send with the PoC
